The LEGO project was one of my first class projects. This project consisted of modeling many types of Lego's and 3d printing the Lego's out to make a creation. I modeled Lego pieces that exist and created new pieces that did not exist. I came up with the idea of a McDonald's when I got McDonald's before school. I looked for inspiration online and decided to do the iconic McDonald's roof. The restaurant uses more rectangular pieces, while the roof uses slant pieces. This project taught me how to use many skills we have learned and put them together. We used 3d printing, concept sketches and Fusion 360. Setting goals for myself and dividing the project into many parts helped me complete the project
